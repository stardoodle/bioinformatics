THE SCIENCE

Membrane Folding
Membrane proteins fold differently because the bilayer is a "fatty" environment.

HYDROPHOBICITY

Non-polar residues (Leu, Ile) stay inside the membrane to hide from water. This is the Hydrophobic Effect.

STRUCTURE PREDICTION

We use Hydropathy Plots to predict which parts of a protein sequence will sit inside the lipid layer.
