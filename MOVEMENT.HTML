THE SCIENCE

UNIT I: Structure Determination
Scientists use different tools based on how heavy or still a protein is.
SOLUBILITY
If it's liquid, use magnets (NMR). If it's a rock-hard crystal, use radiation (X-ray).
RESOLUTION
X-ray gives the highest detail, but Cryo-EM is better for huge protein complexes.
