THE SCIENCE

Amino Acid Propensity
Prediction algorithms look for "Helix Lovers" and "Sheet Lovers" based on their side-chain chemistry.

PREDICTION RULES

We use Propensity Scales (like Chou-Fasman). For example, Alanine (A) is the king of Helices because its side chain is small and fits perfectly!

HELIX BREAKERS

Proline (P) is a "Breaker" because its ring structure creates a kink that snaps the helix. That's why you Flush them!
