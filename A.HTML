THE SCIENCE

Direct Readout
Proteins recognize DNA by "reading" the chemical signatures of bases inside the Major Groove.

WHAT IS IT?

Binding specificity! Just like your target code, proteins look for specific Motifs (sequences) to bind.

WHY IT MATTERS?

Without this specific "Catching," proteins would bind to random DNA, and genes wouldn't turn on correctly!
